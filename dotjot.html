DotJot – Your Flexible Digital Journal

DotJot brings the simplicity and freedom of a classic dotted notebook planner right to your phone. Capture tasks, track habits, plan goals, jot down thoughts, and organize your life with total customization—no rigid templates, just adaptable tools that work the way you do.

Key Features

  • Unlimited daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly entries
  • Enter tasks, events, notes, and habits effortlessly
  • Five beautiful color themes, including the cozy Classic Brown
  • Fully offline – everything stays local on your device (sync coming soon)
  • Basic habit progress statistics and insights
  • Clean, customizable interface for planning your days your way
  • Dark mode support and accessibility options
