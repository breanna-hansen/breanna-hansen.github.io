About Us

Cima Development is all about turning everyday challenges into elegant mobile solutions. We spot real needs in daily life—whether it’s staying organized, finding moments of calm, celebrating what matters, or learning something new—and build thoughtful, high-quality apps to address them. Every app we create is something we personally use, love, and believe in, crafted with care to feel intuitive, reliable, and genuinely helpful.

We do what we do because we’re passionate about creating tools that make life a little easier and more enjoyable—one tap at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Cima Development FAQ! Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about our mobile apps, including DotJot, Celebrate Hub, LDS Temple Puzzles, Lingua Learn, beta testing, downloads, and more. If you don’t see your question listed, feel free to reach out via the contact form—we love hearing from users and are happy to help.

What is Cima Development?

Cima Development is an independent mobile app creator focused on building thoughtful, user-friendly apps that solve real everyday problems.

When will my favorite app be released?

DotJot is the closest to launch—aiming for Google Play in mid-February 2026. The others are in active development and will follow shortly after.

How do I become a beta tester?

Join our beta program through the sign-up form linked in the footer or on the DotJot page. Android users with a Gmail address are especially welcome—beta testers often receive free premium access later.

Are the apps free?

Yes, all apps are free to download and use with core features. Some (like DotJot) will offer optional premium upgrades for extra customization and advanced tools.

Can I suggest features or give feedback?

Absolutely! We actively use user feedback to improve our apps. Share your ideas through the contact form, social media, or during beta testing.

Do you make custom apps for clients?

No—we focus exclusively on creating and improving our own original apps.

How can I stay updated on new releases?

Follow us on Instagram (@cima.development), TikTok (@cima.development), or Facebook for launch announcements, sneak peeks, and beta opportunities.

Where can I download the apps?

Where can I download the apps?
All of our apps are (or will be) available on both Google Play for Android devices and the App Store for iPhone and iPad. Check each app’s dedicated section on this site for the direct download links once they’re live.

I have a question not listed here—what should I do?

Just send us a message through the contact form—we usually reply within 24–48 hours.
