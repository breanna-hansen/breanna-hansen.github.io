Celebrate Hub - Build Meaningful Moments Together

Celebrate Hub is being built to help families and individuals organize birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, achievements, and everyday milestones in one uplifting space. The goal is simple: make it easier to remember what matters and send meaningful celebration messages on time.

Instead of promising fully automatic outbound texting, Celebrate Hub focuses on what works reliably on Android and iOS: smart reminders, prebuilt custom messages, and one-tap sharing flows that help users complete the send quickly.

Planned Features

  • Recurring birthday and milestone tracking with flexible reminder timing
  • Push notifications on celebration days and optional advance reminders
  • Custom message templates users can personalize by recipient and tone
  • Celebration card creator with text and image designs ready to share
  • One-tap handoff to messaging and social apps through native share actions
  • Send-status tracking so users can mark celebrations completed
  • Clean, family-friendly interface designed for quick daily use
