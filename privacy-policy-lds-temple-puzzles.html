LDS Temple Puzzles Privacy Policy

Overview

LDS Temple Puzzles ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use our mobile application (the "App").

What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data, limited to information stored locally on your device:

  • Puzzle completion progress
  • User preferences (sound, music, haptics settings)
  • Premium status if applicable

Local Data Storage

All your personal data (puzzle progress, preferences, and settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync or back up your device through your phone's system settings.

Installation Tracking

When you download the App from a shared link, we use OneLink attribution technology to track that you installed the App from that link. This helps us understand which promotional channels are effective. OneLink collects basic information such as your device type, country, and installation date. This data is not connected to personal information that identifies you.

We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Browsing behavior or analytics
  • Biometric data
  • Payment or financial information

Third-Party Services

Our App uses Expo for development and deployment. Expo may collect basic diagnostic information to improve the App's stability and performance. No personal data is shared with third parties for marketing or tracking purposes beyond the OneLink attribution technology described above.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

Your Rights

You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App. You can also manage your App preferences, including sound and music settings, through the App's Settings screen.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or other reasonable means.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Help section of the App.

Non-Affiliation Disclaimer

LDS Temple Puzzles is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or an official product of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
