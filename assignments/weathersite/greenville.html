Greenville Hosts Bane County Fair

The Bane County Fair in Greenville opened today with high attendance due to warm weather and partly cloudy skies. Fair attendees enjoyed the respite from rain during the last week to sample dozens of varieties of food, games, rides, and shows. With high temperature's for the week expected to be in the 80's and several popular musicians scheduled, Greenville is set to have a banner year for attendance.

Tickets for the fair can be purchased at local Bixby stores or at the fair entrance. Individual tickets are $5 each or $20 per family. An all inclusive wristbrand ride pass is available for $30 at the gate for those 48" or taller. For those under 48", ride wristbands are $20. Concert tickets for the John Michael Montgomery, Derby Crash Cars, and Tru Rock shows can be purchased online at countyticketmaster.com or at the gate entrance. All seats are first come first served.

Several food eating contests are scheduled throughout the week. The annual Bee Berry Pie Eating Contest will be held at the small grandstand at 7:00 p.m. on Wednesday night. The Big Buns Hot Dog Eating Contest is accepting registrations through 5:00 p.m. on Friday with the contest set to start at 5:30. Each contest offers age group contests and has limited registration so sign up early!
