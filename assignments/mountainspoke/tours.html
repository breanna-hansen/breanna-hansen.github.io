Tours

Mountain Spoke Bike Tours are a great way to experience all that Bozeman has to offer! Join us on a Mountain Spoke Pub Bike or Farmer's Market Tuk Tuk Limo tour.

Pub Bike Tour

Price: $30/person

  • Skill Level: Beginner +
  • Length: 2 miles and takes 3 hours
  • Number of Riders: The pub bike requires a minimum of 6 pedalers and seats up to 15 people.
  • Location:Tours depart from the parking lot at the corner of Valley Commons Drive and E. Mendenhall Street.
  • Time: 5:30 - 8:30 p.m.

Farmer's Market Tour

Price: $90 plus $20/person

  • Skill Level: Beginner +
  • Length: 2 miles and takes 2 - 2.5 hours
  • Number of Riders: The tuk tuk limo seats up to 6.
  • Location:Tours depart from the parking lot at the corner of Valley Commons Drive and E. Mendenhall Street.
  • Time: 6:15 - 9:15 p.m.
