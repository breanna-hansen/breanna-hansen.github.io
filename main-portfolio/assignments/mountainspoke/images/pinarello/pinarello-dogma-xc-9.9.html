Dogma XC 9.9 Carbon 60HM1K Torayca®XC16

Copyright © Cicli Pinarello SRL

You thought that everything had already been invented... right?

The traditional frames have the rear stays directly connected to the top tube, this means that every vibration coming from the rear triangle is directly transferred to the top tube and often to the handlebar, thus compromising comfort and handling.
We have divided the two main triangles, moving the rear triangle higher.
This solution is associated with the asymmetric bifurcated rear stays, drastically reducing the shock from the rear for improved handling and stability.
This is the meaning of ONDA XC Twin Arms Asymmetric.
